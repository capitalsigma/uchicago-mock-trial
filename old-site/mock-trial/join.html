JOINING

So you've decided to join! We couldn't be happier! (Actually, we could be, especially when we win championships and stuff. But besides for that.) Send an email to our President or any of our other executive officers and they'll tell you about our application process for this year.

Generally, this will consist of three steps.

  1. Attend our open house! This year's open house will be held at at

    Thursday, September 26 2013, 4:00-5:00pm
    Hallowed Grounds

  2. Attend our info sessions! This session is designed to demonstrate the conduct of a typical mock trial and prepare you for our auditions. There are two information sessions scheduled this year:
    Sunday, September 29th, 2013, 1:00-3:00pm
    Harper 130

    Saturday October 5th, 2013, 11:00am-12:00pm
    Bartlett Lounge

  3. Audition for Mock Trial! During the audition there will be opportunities for you to show our Board your strengths in either the witness and attorney roles. All members are given the opportunity to compete in the fall season (barring an unforeseen spike in interest).

Once all this is done, congratulations! Feel the love you get from being a UChicago mocker!

UChicago Mock Trial is a competitive activity that combines law, debate, and acting. Team members build a case as both attorneys and witnesses, and travel to tournaments across the country. Contact an officer to find out more about joining!
