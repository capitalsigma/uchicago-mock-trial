WHAT DOES MOCK TRIAL DO?

Every year, top schools from all over the country send teams to compete in The American Mock Trial Association's competitions. Teams are made up of 6-10 undergraduates who travel the country competing in invitational, regional, and national trials. Whether it's a civil or criminal case, every school presents both the defense and the plaintiff or prosecution case in competition against other colleges. Part acting, part arguing, and part oration, Mock Trial gives participants the chance to play the hot-shot attorney or the dramatic witness. Whether you want a career in the law or not, Mock Trial is a great opportunity to exercise your competitive edge, and generally have a good time!
