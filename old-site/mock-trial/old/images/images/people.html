The 2010-2011 competition season is, to the regret and heartbreak of many a mocker, over. Come back in October 2011, right after selections, for new team information!

Mock Trial at UChicago is an award-winning program that trains its members in the arts of rhetoric, argument, and even acting.

We act as attorneys and witnesses in both criminal and civil trials, and prepare cases for both the prosecution/plaintiff and defense.
