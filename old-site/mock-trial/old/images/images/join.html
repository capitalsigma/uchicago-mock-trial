JOINING

So you've decided to join! We couldn't be happier! (Actually, we could be, especially when we win championships and stuff. But besides for that.) Send an email to our President (ermoore@uchicago.edu) or any of our other executive officers and they'll tell you about our application process for this year.

Generally, this will consist of two steps.

  1. Attend an information session on Mock Trial. If you are new to Mock Trial, this session is designed to demonstrate the conduct of a typical mock trial and prepare you for our auditions held about a week or so from the info session. We do, however, try to allow as many people to take part in Mock Trial as possible.
  2. Audition for Mock Trial! During the audition there will be opportunities for you to show our Board your strengths in either the witness or attorney roles. You may also indicate your preference for either role on our forms. We try to grant members their preferences, but this may not always be possible, since we have limited positions.

Once all this is done, congratulations! Feel the love you get from being a UChicago mocker!

Mock Trial at UChicago is an award-winning program that trains its members in the arts of rhetoric, argument, and even acting.

We act as attorneys and witnesses in both criminal and civil trials, and prepare cases for both the prosecution/plaintiff and defense.
