Approaching Different Witness Types
vSobbing Widow
–compassionate & level tone, firm yet not overbearing, sympathy
vDefendant
–varies, but usually be very firm, watch for invention/impeachment points, aggressive if situation warrants
vEye Witness
–pin them to exactly what they saw, may have to be careful if eye witness is very emotional
vRespectable Expert
–ask technical expert-like questions, challenge expertise on issue, cross on background (usually some mess-up in past)
vPeculiar Expert
–ask technical expert-like questions & question witness' expertise even more, cross on background (always some previous mess-up)
vImpartial fact witness
–pin them to their affidavit, might also have to be careful if witness is too emotional
vKooky fact witness
–usually can attack methods, character or credibility
