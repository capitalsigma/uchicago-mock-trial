Do not be indignant
v“It is all right for you once in a while to act indignant. But never be indignant”
v -Judge Learned Hand
v
vNever get angry with any witness no matter what they do
v
vLetting your anger show will only hurt your score on cross-examination
v
vIt is much better to proceed calmly and respectfully in establishing how the witness has “misbehaved”
