Avoid asking one question too many
vNever argue during cross-examination
v
vLay out the facts and admissions that you need for your argument
v
vNever attempt to get the witness to agree with your characterization of a certain situation
v
vNever ask any witness to explain anything ever!
v
vNo attorney should ever utter the words “How” or “Why” on cross-examination
v
vIf you lay out your questions correctly, the facts you elicit will argue for you
