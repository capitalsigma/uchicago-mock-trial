2009-2010 Case Summary

In 2002, Jacob Bennett, Jackie Owens, and Casey Maxwell joined forces to create a major movie studio, Trifecta Entertainment. After some initial growing pains, Trifecta was about to embark on its most profitable year. But then, on June 17th, 2008, the police received a call reporting the disappearance of one of the partners, Jacob Bennett. Foul play was suspected, and the police discovered the missing partner’s corpse buried at the base of Calkins Cliffs. The two remaining partners have been indicted for murder.
